Kmart Express store types: code meanings

Code Meaning
FULL Full convenience store, seperate from the gas station (Think Wawa or stores operated by EG America)
FULL W PENSKE FULL, plus an attached Penske Express auto center
KIOSK No convenience store, just a small "kiosk" for the attendant (Think BJ's or Costco)
CANOPYSTR Limited convenience store under the pump canopy (Think Giant or Kroger)
